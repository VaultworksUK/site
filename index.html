Core Idea

Vaultworks provides Vault360 — a secure, client-facing platform that law firms offer to their clients.

It brings together life admin, financial information, and estate planning into one encrypted digital vault, reducing complexity today and burden tomorrow.

Problem

When it matters most, critical information is often the hardest to find.

The result is delay, stress, and unnecessary cost at the worst possible time.

Important documents are scattered across emails, paper files, and devices

Families are left searching, guessing, and under pressure

Executors face weeks of admin before they can act

Law firms lose visibility and continuity after wills are completed

Solution

Vault360 gives individuals a single, secure place to organise and manage their life.

Store key documents and personal data securely
Record assets, liabilities, and important accounts
Capture wishes, instructions, and key contacts
Share access safely with partners, executors, and advisors
Enable controlled release of information when required

All protected with client-side encryption and designed for simplicity.
